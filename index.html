🌿 Atmospheric CO₂ — Keeling Curve i
Keeling Curve
Weekly CO₂ readings from Mauna Loa volcano (Hawaii) since 1958. The sawtooth pattern = forests breathing with seasons. The long-term rise = fossil fuels. 420 ppm today vs 316 ppm in 1960 — +33% in 65 years.
Source: NOAA GML · gml.noaa.gov/ccgg · Weekly
