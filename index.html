Aggregate of 34 independent signals across 9 domains — from space to oceans, internet traffic to migration flows. Score 100 = calm world, 0 = extreme stress.

⚠️ Signals under stress
Normalisation methodology
Historical percentile
Each series compared against its own 5-year history. BDI at 1400 and Kp at 7 become comparable via percentile ranking.
p = rank(v) / N × 100
Inverted polarity
Signals "bad when rising" (conflicts, pollution, food prices) are inverted so 100 = always calm.
score = 100 − percentile(v)
Weighted aggregation
Category score = weighted average of signals. EPI = average of 9 categories, conflict & disaster overweighted.
EPI = Σ(wᵢ × cᵢ) / Σwᵢ
